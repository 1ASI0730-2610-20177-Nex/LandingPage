Pricing

Plans designed around your smart home needs

Nex follows a freemium approach: begin with a free plan to experience centralized control and basic energy insights, then upgrade as you need deeper analytics, smarter alerts, and advanced reporting. All prices are shown in Peruvian soles (PEN) and are approximate—they may vary due to taxes, IGV, or promotional offers.

Free

Ideal for beginners and users who want simple control without any commitment.

S/ 0 / month

  • Register up to 5 devices in your home
  • Room-based organization for better device management
  • Basic dashboards with live status and recent activity
  • Energy overview with consumption trends from the past 7 days
  • Mobile-optimized interface for easy access anywhere
  • Email support with standard response times

Premium

Designed for users who want deeper insights, automation, and smarter decision-making.

S/ 15 / month monthly billing · cancel anytime

  • All Free features plus higher or unlimited device capacity
  • Extended data history to analyze long-term usage patterns
  • Custom alerts for unusual activity or consumption spikes
  • Reports & exports in formats like CSV and PDF
  • Priority support with faster response times
  • Early access to new features and integrations

Nex is still evolving—features listed reflect our planned roadmap. Initial versions may include simulated data while full device integrations are being developed.
